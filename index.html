Apps

I've tinkered around with android apps and Android Studio a bit now. In the 7th grade, I made 2 apps using MIT's App Inventor, and they're both available on the Google Play Store if you wish to check them out. Feel free to make any suggestions or comment here.

  • Spell Bind
  • Education
  • Practice spelling words by choosing any level of difficulty between Grade 1 and Grade 8. Comes pre-installed with 750 words, and has the ability to add more words.
  • Learn Mode: list of words + pronounciation
  • Test Mode: keep score of correct spellings and keep track of constantly misspelled words
  • 100+ installs

  • Free

    no ads
  • Download
  • Topcorn
  • Game - Casual
  • Multiplayer app for the popular party game Popcorn. When all players' devices are connected to a single game room, start the game. Start at 0 and tap the screen to go up by 1. The goal is to reach the highest number possible, but if multiple users tap the screen at the same time, the number resets to 0.
  • 4 public gamerooms
  • Ability to create private gamerooms with access key.
  • 10+ installs

  • Free

    no ads
  • Download
